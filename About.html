About ChristSaves

Launched in 2020, ChristSaves is a dedicated platform for
fast spreading of the gospel. We facilitate God's word to
reach as many souls as possible around the world through rich christian materials.

Our Mission

As part of the ChristSaves Group, our mission is to make it easy to access God's word anywhere.

We do this by giving God's shepherds a platform necessary to reach a global
audience for administering the word, and by helping seekers of the word find
different great materials to build their faith in God.

Anytime, Anywhere

As a platform, we continue to develop services to help
christianity grow and reach great heights.

Whether it's receiving anointing materials from your mobile phone or contacting
God's shepherds for personal guidance, turn to ChristSaves for all your
salvation needs.
